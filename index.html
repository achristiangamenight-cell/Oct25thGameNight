Christian Singles Game Night — Agenda

October 25 • Sol Vermella • 4 Vermella Wy, Union, NJ 07083

Venue open: 5:30 PM • Start: 6:00 PM • Wrap: 10:00 PM

  1. 5:30–6:00 PM

    Check-In & Mingle

    Name tags, potluck drop-off, BYOB table, music. Give guests colored stickers or wristbands for table rotations.

  2. 6:00–6:10 PM

    Welcome & House Rules

    Host welcome, open prayer, code of conduct, schedule overview.

  3. 6:10–6:25 PM

    Icebreaker

    “Human Bingo” or “Two Truths & a Lie.” Goal: meet five new people.

  4. 6:25–7:05 PM

    Game Block 1 (40 min)

    Tables: UNO, Spades, Dominoes, Charades, party game. Captains teach rules.

  5. 7:05–7:20 PM

    Potluck Break #1 (15 min)

    Half of guests serve; others jump into quick mini-rounds.

  6. 7:20–8:00 PM

    Game Block 2 (40 min)

    Rotate by color; mix new groups for fresh conversations.

  7. 8:00–8:10 PM

    Photo & Outfit Shout-Outs

    Group photo (honor no-photo opt-outs) and highlight best “Old School Attire.”

  8. 8:10–8:50 PM

    Game Block 3 (40 min)

    Winners advance to finals while casual tables keep the fun flowing.

  9. 8:50–9:05 PM

    Potluck Break #2 / Dessert

    Swap serving order from Break #1, highlight dessert table.

  10. 9:05–9:35 PM

    Finals & Speed Social

    Finals spotlight plus three 5-minute rotating table prompts for everyone else.

  11. 9:35–9:50 PM

    Prizes & Announcements

    Celebrate best outfit, tournament winners, best potluck dish, and next event date.

  12. 9:50–10:00 PM

    Closing & Thank-You

    Prayer and quick clean-up instructions.

  13. 10:00–10:20 PM

    Clean-Up

    Volunteers reset the space and pack up supplies.
